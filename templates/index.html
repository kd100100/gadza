About Us

Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian Economy"- said Mahatma Gandhi, 6 decades ago. Agriculture Sector contributes 6.4 percent of total world's economic production. With 1.3 billion people employed in the sector, agriculture is the second greatest source of employment worldwide after services and it accounts 28% of global employment. One of the most important component in agriculture is trading.
To make it easy, secure, and healthy, Gazda is formed. Gazda is an online platform for exchange of agricultural produce from Farmers to Retailers. Gazda helps in discovering better prices and provides facilities for smooth marketing of their produce. Gazda also is targeting the elimination of middlemen so that farmers can also get a better deal for themselves by selling fruits and vegetables directly to wholesalers and retailers.
